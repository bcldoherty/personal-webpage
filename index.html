Brandon Doherty

I am a Math PhD student at the University of Western Ontario working under the supervision of Chris Kapulkin.

My main areas of research are in categorical homotopy theory and higher category theory. My most recently completed project was the construction of a model structure on the category of cubical sets which models the theory of (∞,1)-categories. I am currently working on other projects applying cubical sets to higher category theory. In the past, I have also worked on formalization of category theory in the UniMath library.
